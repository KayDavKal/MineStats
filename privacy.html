Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 19.12.2024 6.31pm UTC+1

1. Data Collection

MineStats does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

The Bot operates exclusively on Discord and processes commands in real-time without saving user or server information.

2. Third-Party APIs

MineStats uses the following APIs to fetch Minecraft-related data:

  • api.mcstatus.io
  • api.mojang.com
  • api.mineatar.io

These APIs are used to gather publicly available information about Minecraft servers and players. MineStats does not control or assume responsibility for how these third parties handle your data.

3. Cookies and Tracking

The MineStats website does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking mechanisms.

4. Age Restrictions

The Bot is intended for users aged 13 or older, in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service. We do not knowingly interact with users below this age.

5. User Rights

Since MineStats does not store any data, users do not need to request data deletion. Any data processed through the Bot is handled in real-time and not retained.

6. Contact

For any concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via:

  • Our Discord support server.
  • Email: minestats@mcbehub.de.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as necessary. Changes will be posted on the website, and continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
